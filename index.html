About the game

Capir is a puzzle game where you rotate groups of pieces to match a solution.

To rotate the pieces, press on the first one and release on the last in line.


Capir has two game modes: challenge mode and free mode.

Challenge mode

Each puzzle in challenge mode is custom-made, with moves that have been carefully selected to ensure a challenging yet solvable experience.

The puzzles can be solved in 3, 4, or 5 moves, depending on the difficulty level.

3 4 5

Pieces in challenge mode have dots that hint how many times they should be rotated.

Free mode

In free mode, each puzzle is randomly generated, and the number of moves required to solve it is not predetermined.

The best scores are recorded on a global leaderboard. Compete against the world!

Unlocks

Multiple features unlock as you complete puzzles in the game:

  • Challenge mode is unlocked after finishing the tutorial.
  • In challenge mode, you must solve three puzzles in each row to unlock the next.
  • Free mode unlocks after finishing a number of challenge puzzles.
  • As you solve free mode levels, new boards will unlock for you to choose.
