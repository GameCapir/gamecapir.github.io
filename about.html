About

Capir is a puzzle game where you rotate groups of tiles to match the solution.

To rotate the tiles you press on the first tile of the group move to the last tile and release.

You can only rotate groups of tiles that are aligned.

You have a limited number of moves to solve the puzzle.

Game modes

Capir has two game modes:

Challenge mode

The puzzles are custom made challenges, where the moves to solve the puzzle have been decided beforehand.

Challenge mode puzzles can be solved in 3, 4, or 5 moves, depending on the difficulty level.

The pieces in challenge mode show a number of dots, that hint how many times you should rotate a tile.

Free mode

Each puzzle is randomly generated and the amount of moves to solve them is not decided.

The best scores in Free mode are recorded on a global leaderboard, you compete against the world!
